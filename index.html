Frequently Asked Questions

What are the emergency hours at Eranad Hospital?
Our emergency and trauma services run 24 hours a day, 7 days a week (Monday–Sunday) to ensure patients get critical attention immediately.
Which pediatricians and cardiologists consult here?
Our child care unit is headed by Dr. Shaheer M (MBBS, DNB-Pediatrics) and our heart department is led by Dr. Anwar K (MBBS, MD, DNB, DrNB Cardiology).
Do you have facilities for ultrasound scanning daily?
Yes, we offer advanced diagnostic ultrasound scanning services daily, supporting cardiology, maternal, and pediatric imaging.
Is the hospital wheelchair accessible?
Absolutely. We have step-free main entrance and exit access, wheelchair-friendly parking slots, and support digital contactless card payments.
